Legal Protections for Women in India

Know your rights under the Indian Penal Code (IPC), IT Act, and other laws. Knowledge is power.

Indian Penal Code (IPC)

IPC 354Physical Safety

Assault / Outraging Modesty

Whoever assaults or uses criminal force to any woman, intending to outrage or knowing it likely to outrage her modesty.

Up to 2 years imprisonment + fine
IPC 354ASexual Harassment

Sexual Harassment

Physical contact with sexual overtones, demand for sexual favours, showing pornography against will, or making sexually coloured remarks.

Up to 3 years + fine
IPC 354DStalking

Stalking

Following, contacting, or monitoring a woman's internet/email use against her explicit indication of disinterest. Repeat offences carry higher penalties.

1st offence: 3 years; Repeat: 5 years
IPC 376Sexual Violence

Rape

Sexual intercourse without consent. Post-2013 Criminal Law Amendment covers a wider definition including penetration under force, threat, or incapacity.

Minimum 10 years to life imprisonment
IPC 498ADomestic Violence

Cruelty by Husband/Relatives

Cruelty to a woman by her husband or his relatives, including physical, mental, or dowry-related harassment.

Up to 3 years + fine
IPC 509Verbal Abuse

Insulting Modesty of Woman

Words, gestures, or acts intended to insult the modesty of any woman, including catcalling, lewd comments, or obscene gestures in public.

Up to 3 years + fine

Information Technology (IT) Act

IT Act 66CCyber Crime

Identity Theft

Fraudulently or dishonestly making use of any person's electronic signature, password, or unique identification feature.

Up to 3 years + ₹1 lakh fine
IT Act 66ECyber Crime

Violation of Privacy

Intentionally capturing, publishing, or transmitting image of private area of any person without consent in circumstances violating privacy.

Up to 3 years + ₹2 lakh fine
IT Act 67ACyber Crime

Sexually Explicit Content Online

Publishing or transmitting sexually explicit act or conduct in electronic form. Includes morphed images, revenge porn, and non-consensual sharing.

Up to 5 years + ₹10 lakh fine

Cyber Safety Resources & Reporting Portals

Official portals and tools for reporting cybercrime, protecting your digital identity, and staying safe online.

National Cybercrime Reporting Portal

Official Government of India portal to report cybercrimes including online harassment, financial fraud, fake profiles, and more.

cybercrime.gov.in

CERT-In (Computer Emergency Response Team)

India's nodal agency for cybersecurity incidents. Report phishing, malware, website vulnerabilities, and data breaches.

cert-in.org.in

SHeBox — Sexual Harassment at Workplace

Online complaint portal for women employees (government and private) to report sexual harassment at the workplace under POSH Act.

shebox.nic.in

Have I Been Pwned?

Check if your email address or phone number has been exposed in a known data breach. Take action to secure compromised accounts.

haveibeenpwned.com

StopNCII — Non-Consensual Intimate Images

International tool to prevent non-consensual sharing of intimate images. Generate a hash of your photo without uploading it to stop its spread.

stopncii.org

Cyber Crime Helpline

For immediate assistance with cybercrime including online fraud, harassment, stalking, and identity theft. Available 24/7 nationwide.

Call 1930
